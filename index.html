About Us

The Science and Technology club of Techno Main Salt Lake - Geekonix, was established in September 2005 to inspire young minds and promote unconventional and out-of-the-box thinking. Ever since its inception, Geekonix has been paving the way for bright and zealous enthusiasts of technology, helping them innovate and create all the while learning important management skills.

Inception

The commencement of Geekonix began with a community of like-minded students discussing their thoughtful ideas about software and projects through the forum of common chat in a Yahoo Group. The official introductory sessions as conducted by the senior members of the club made its presence felt outside the group as well as in college too. Eventually with the growing spirit of enthusiasm as more than 25 people were asked to join the chat forum, they also supposedly expressed their interest to join the club, and indeed, most of them ended up playing vital roles in the growth curve of Geekonix and its brainchild EDGE.

Ideation

What can happen if a group of members get accumulated in a computer lab along with some Geeks to just talk and ideate? Well, that's what led to EDGE, the first-ever independent Technological Fest of our college in 2005, although being a part of Anakhronos, our cultural fest. Support flourished through the young minds making it possible for a first grand EDGE'07. The popularity led to the foundation of our very first website for EDGE'07-THINK BEYOND.

Execution

“Innovation is rewarded. Execution is worshipped.” - Eric Thomas From struggling to approach companies for sponsorship to getting permission from the authority- the journey from ideation to inception to execution was never a cakewalk. EDGE, the brainchild of many, started its journey in the year 2007 with the tagline “Think Beyond”. Every challenge, every adversity was the seed of opportunity and growth. The team with their indomitable spirit brought in sponsors, first of those times when all the fests completely relied on college funds. The fest was held on weekends to not disturb the normal college schedule with no access to infrastructure. EDGE had to rent its server, buy its own domain name (at a time when getting a credit card was not easy). After overcoming all hurdles, the first website was launched at edg.co.in. ... As time proceeded, EDGE became a brand- a brand everyone looks up to now. The never-ending zeal to do better still continues.

Journey so far

Swirling obstacles around the corner and behind, with every passing year, the club has risen to glory with each iteration. From launching their first website edg.co.in to organizing 40+ events in a 3-day fest, EDGE has only risen above echelons with surpassing time. Since EDGE’10, the most-talked night of the town EDGE Night came into play which has witnessed famous stand-up comics like Rahul Subramaniam, The Viral Fever, Sahil Shah, Zakir Khan, Sorabh Pant, and many more through the years. Along with it, EDGE Talks has brought us nationwide famous speakers and celebrities like RJ Praveen, Yahya Bootwala, and a lot more. In 2018, The Smart India Hackathon organized by the Central Government of India in collaboration with Techno India Group and Geekonix, by then the officially established science and technology club of Techno Main Salt Lake ... provided the members of the club to gain more exposure and eventually convoked its brainchild EDGE to rise higher than the horizon. In 2016, besides the technical facet of reaching greatness the club also started to focus on the aspects of the society and its need through conducting programs like Walkathon, Orphanage Visit, Cleanliness Drive, etc. under the bureau of EDGE Deeds and in the same year, the 10th iteration of EDGE was labelled as the second-largest Technical Fest in Eastern India with a footfall of about 7000 students participating in it and winning prize money of about 7 lakhs. With exciting events of robotics and gaming, EDGE has sought to bring extravaganza every time. To sum it up, Geekonix has only witnessed a rise at a cosmic rate and will only work towards reaching the zenith.

CLUBS

CUrBrain

The official coding club of Geekonix, CUrBrain is the befitting place for passionate coders to outstand their capability in flawless coding. Through various workshops and competitions held by the club nurturing the trailing path of coding skills, students are prepared to experience and achieve excellence in effective problem solving through programming.

Robopalz Robotics Clubs

Robopalz Robotics Club, the official robotics club of Geekonix is where robot enthusiasts find their home. Via a series of conducted workshops related to both automatically and manually functioning robots, the club encourages enthusiasts to participate in competitions and showcase their knowledge in artificial intelligence operating through machine learning.

InFocus

Moments are transformed into memories through the art of photography and for people who love to capture time and objects standing still in the frame, InFocus, the photography club under Geekonix is their port of call. The club conducts workshops and events to help the photography enthusiasts develop and excel in the art of the same.

Cyber Crusade

For some, gaming is not just a hobby, it's a passion. Such passionate young minds led to the foundation of Cyber Crusade. Hosting a plethora of events every year, Cyber Crusade welcomes you to a healthy competition.

CIIC Money Matters

Changing market scenario demands an innovative mind in terms of entrepreneurship. CIIC is that platform that aims at embracing such creative and innovative perspectives through entrepreneurial activities.

Elevation

Want to design a bridge of your own? Elevation provides just the platform for using your analytics and problem-solving skills to shape up a bridge using CAD software or to create a simple model design of it.

Newron

“Anything's possible if you've got enough nerve.” For good ideas and true innovation, you need human interaction, conflict, argument, and debate. Newron, the official Quiz, Debate, and Electronics club of Geekonix, gives a platform to present your views and showcase your ideas which have the power to bring a change in the world.

Food for Fun

Food is our common ground, a universal experience. Food for Fun, the club under Geekonix is the perfect blend of food, technology, and fun. It gives us the proper insight into the world of food and allows students to come up with innovative and sustainable solutions.

Creat-It & Innovati

Are you ready to tickle your brains for some creativity meshed with scientific technology and wit? Then hop into the grand platform provided by Create It or Innovati, turning your ideas into reality.

Geek Designs

Design is the intermediary between information and understanding. This newly launched club under Geekonix aims at giving a platform to the creative minds to paint their thoughts and explore the world of art and animation.
